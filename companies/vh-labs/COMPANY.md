# VH Labs

VH Labs is an agency and fulfillment company that manages client growth across marketing services, creative production, funnel building, media buying, and technology. It operates as a subsidiary of Emuna, alongside Acquisition Systems (performance marketing/affiliate network) and Attora (e-commerce revenue activation).

## AI Agent Team

Six AI agents support the human team:

1. **Marketing Manager** — Owns client growth outcomes, manages campaign strategy, coordinates Creative Director, Copywriter, and Backend Marketer agents. Reports to Casey Amundson.
2. **Creative Director** — Produces creative briefs and tracks creative performance per client. Reports to Marketing Manager.
3. **Copywriter** — Writes direct-response copy across all formats, adapting to each client's brand voice. Reports to Marketing Manager.
4. **Backend Marketer** — Owns post-conversion flows: email, SMS, upsells, retention automation. Reports to Marketing Manager.
5. **Developer** — Builds and maintains tech stack, API integrations, and troubleshoots issues. Reports to Matt Payne.
6. **Customer Service** — First point of contact for client customers, manages tickets and knowledge base. Reports to Casey Amundson.

## Operating Principles

- Every campaign starts with a measurable goal tied to client revenue.
- Own the client outcome, not just the deliverable. If metrics are down, diagnose the full funnel.
- Delegate with clear briefs — never hand off ambiguity.
- Creative exists to convert. Research what works in each client's vertical.
- Differentiate every client. Cookie-cutter templates are how agencies lose clients.
- The backend is where the real margin lives. Build flows that maximize LTV from every lead.
- Reliability over cleverness. Tech must work 24/7 because campaigns run 24/7.
- Customer service is a profit center. Every support interaction is a retention opportunity.
- Review performance daily, report weekly, strategize monthly.
- Data first, then action. Diagnose before reacting.
