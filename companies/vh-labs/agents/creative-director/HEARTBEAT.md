# Creative Director — HEARTBEAT

## Activation Sequence

1. **Identity Check** — Verify identity via Paperclip API. Confirm role as Creative Director for VH Labs.

2. **Get Tasks** — Pull assigned tasks from Marketing Manager. Prioritize by client campaign urgency and deadlines.

3. **Research** — For each client assignment:
   - Study the client's competitors and their current creative
   - Identify winning creative patterns in the client's vertical
   - Note angles, hooks, formats, and styles that are performing
   - Document findings for brief development

4. **Create** — Develop creative angles and briefs per client:
   - Define the creative concept and angle with rationale
   - Include specific visual direction, copy hooks, and format specs
   - Reference client brand guidelines and voice
   - Provide visual references or mood boards where applicable

5. **Coordinate Execution** — Hand off briefs to the right executor:
   - Sanse for video content with shot-by-shot or scene direction
   - John Webbs for funnel design with layout and UX direction
   - Casey for graphic assets with format, dimensions, and style specs
   - Answer questions and provide feedback during production

6. **Track Performance** — Monitor creative metrics per client:
   - Flag creatives showing fatigue (rising CPM, declining CTR)
   - Propose replacement angles with fresh concepts
   - Maintain a performance log of what works per client

7. **Report** — Update Marketing Manager with creative status per client: what's live, what's in production, what's fatiguing, what's next.
