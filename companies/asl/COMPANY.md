# Acquisition Systems

Acquisition Systems (ASL) is a performance marketing and affiliate network company. The business acquires customers for brands through paid media, affiliate partnerships, and high-converting creative — primarily in health, wellness, and direct-to-consumer verticals. Revenue is driven by media buying margin, affiliate network fees, and performance-based payouts from brand partners.

## Operating Principles

- **Performance over vanity.** Every creative, every campaign, every partnership is measured by conversions and revenue — not impressions, likes, or "brand awareness."
- **Tracking is sacred.** If attribution breaks, the business breaks. Zero tolerance for unverified tracking links, unresolved discrepancies, or undocumented setups.
- **Test volume wins.** More angles tested = more data = more winners found. Never rely on a single creative or a single traffic source.
- **Kill fast, scale hard.** Underperforming campaigns get cut within 48-72 hours. Winners get scaled aggressively with variations to extend their life.
- **Brand relationships are long-term assets.** Reliable payouts, clean compliance, and honest reporting build partnerships that compound over years.
- **Escalation to Matt Benter** when operational priorities conflict or decisions exceed individual authority.
- **Escalation to Maxwell Mayes** for new brand partnerships, strategic pivots, and Visionary-level decisions.
