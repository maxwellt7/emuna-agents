# Attora

Attora is an e-commerce revenue activation company. We take products and turn them into live, converting offers — DTC funnels, paid media creatives, VSLs, email sequences, and landing pages. We do not build brands for the sake of branding. We build offers that sell.

## Operating Principles

- **Offers first.** An offer is a price point, a positioning, a funnel path, and a conversion mechanism. Until all four exist, there is no offer.
- **Brief-driven execution.** Every deliverable starts with a brief. A vague brief produces vague work. Briefs are specific enough that builders can execute without guessing.
- **Speed to market.** Launch fast, iterate on data. But never ship a broken funnel — validate before you launch.
- **Creative is a conversion lever.** Every visual choice, every hook, every thumbnail exists to move a metric. Creative is not decoration.
- **Data decides.** Copy, creative, and offers are hypotheses. The market is the judge. Test everything, kill losers fast, scale winners.
- **Nothing ships with errors.** Quality Control is the last line of defense. Every asset matches the brief or it goes back for revision.
- **Escalation protocol.** Operational conflicts escalate to Matt Benter. Visionary-level strategy and big relationships escalate to Maxwell Mayes.
