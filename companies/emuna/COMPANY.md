# Emuna

Emuna is a holding company that owns and operates three subsidiaries:

- **Acquisition Systems (ASL)** — Performance marketing and affiliate network. Media buying, creative production, tracking, and partner management.
- **Attora** — E-commerce revenue activation. SaaS platform, funnels, direct-to-consumer offers, and brand launches.
- **VH Labs** — Agency and fulfillment. Client growth management, marketing services, creative production, and technology.

## Leadership

| Person | Role | Scope |
|--------|------|-------|
| Maxwell Mayes | Visionary | Vision, big ideas, creative problem solving, catalyst, big relationships |
| Matt Benter | Integrator | Execute the vision, own the P&L, filter & prioritize projects, drive processes & consistency, solve issues & bottlenecks |
| Maxwell Mayes | Investor Relations | LMA, create fundraising strategy, identify investors, close investors, provide investor updates |

## Shared Services

Emuna provides centralized services to all subsidiaries:

1. **Finance** — One Financial Director manages payouts, books, payroll, and financial strategy across all entities. Priority: payroll > payouts > reconciliation > reporting > strategy.
2. **Investor Relations** — Two specialized IR agents support Maxwell's fundraising. Alpha handles communications and meetings. Beta handles CRM data and pipeline tracking.

## Operating Principles

- Entity separation: clean financial boundaries between all companies. No commingled funds without documentation.
- Escalation to Matt Benter when cross-entity priorities conflict.
- Escalation to Maxwell Mayes for investor-facing decisions and Visionary-level strategy.
- All investor communications are drafted by AI, reviewed and sent by Maxwell.
- Financial truth over comfort. Numbers are presented as they are, not as we wish they were.
